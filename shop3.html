Kirkens Korshær Genbrug ~ Help Others


If you love vintage treasures or just want to make a great find, the Church Cross Army Recycling store on Vestergade is a must-visit.
This cozy thrift store in Aarhus has everything from men’s and women’s clothing to household items, books, and even used furniture. It’s always fun to explore and find something unexpected.


Discover Hidden Pearls
Whether I’m hunting for seasonal clothes or unique décor, this store never disappoints.
They have a great selection for every occasion—whether it’s outfits for the holidays or special piece of furniture to spruce up my home. I can always count on finding something special among their shelves.


Giving Back to the Community
What makes shopping here even more meaningful is the knowledge that all proceeds go toward supporting vulnerable people in Denmark.
The store is run by dedicated volunteers, and their hard work makes a huge difference.
By shopping or donating, I'm not only supporting a good cause but also helping the environment through recycling.
Plus, if you're looking to volunteer, they’re always happy to welcome more helping hands.
