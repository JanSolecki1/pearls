Red Cross Højbjerg ~ Charity Treasure


I've been visiting this second-hand shop in Højbjerg for years now, and it never disappoints.
Located on the small shopping square at Oddervej, this charming store has a warm, welcoming atmosphere with its white walls and wooden floors.


What to Find There?
The shop offers a great selection of second-hand clothes for men, women, and kids, all in excellent condition.
In addition, I can find shoes, books, toys, and even handmade knitwear from locals.


Charity Work
What I love most is that all proceeds go to a good cause. It's a wonderful way to support the community while finding great treasures.
